About

The Shankara Ghoseva Trust is non-profitable trust.It is started for the purpose to spread awareness about holy cows of Hindu culture in all over Karnataka by conducting lot of related programmes and activities.

Main objectives of the Trust

  1. Construction of Ghoshala
  2. Protection of native cow breeds
  3. More preference to Organic forming
  4. Creating awareness among youths about Ghoseva activities
  5. Foundation of Organization for more Ghoseva activities
  6. Fund collection for Ghoseva
  7. Promotion of info about native cow breeds in social media
  8. Encouragement to native cow raisers
  9. Manufacturing of native cow products
  10. Employment generation for youths
  11. Programs for native culture enhancement
